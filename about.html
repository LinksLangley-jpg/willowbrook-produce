Who We Are

Grown from the ground up, one box at a time

What started as a small stand with a handshake promise to carry only the freshest produce has grown into one of Langley's most trusted local markets. For over 27 years, we've built relationships with farmers across the Fraser Valley and beyond, so what arrives in your bag is never an afterthought.

We are family-owned and family-run. Every crate is reviewed. Every price is honest. When a crop isn't up to standard, it doesn't make the shelf. That's not a policy we wrote down. It's just how we've always operated.

Our customers aren't foot traffic. They're neighbours, regulars, and people who have been coming back season after season because they know they'll leave with something good. Over 118 Google reviews and a 4.6-star average tell you what we can't say ourselves.

What Drives Us

Six values that have never changed

🌾

Farm-First Sourcing

We buy directly from local farmers whenever the season allows. The shorter the chain, the fresher the produce and the more money stays in BC hands.

🤝

Honest Pricing

No markups built on brand names or fancy packaging. Our prices reflect what the produce is worth, not what the market thinks you'll pay.

✅

Quality Control

Every item on the shelf passed someone's eyes. If we wouldn't take it home ourselves, it doesn't go out. Soft, bruised, or off-spec goes back or gets pulled.

🌍

Community Roots

Willowbrook is part of Langley. We sponsor, we show up, and we hire locally. Supporting this market means supporting the broader community around it.

📦

Variety Year-Round

From exotic fruits to everyday staples, we curate our stock with intention. You'll find what you need here, and often discover something new beside it.

💬

Real Relationships

Staff know your face. We remember what you like. That's not a customer service strategy. It's just what happens when you care about the people walking through the door.

Community Impact

Keeping money in the Fraser Valley

Every purchase at Willowbrook Produce has a ripple effect. When we source from local farms, we're helping those families stay viable. When we hire locally and pay fairly, we're investing in Langley. When we price honestly, we make good food accessible to everyone, not just those who can afford a premium.

Over 27 years, that adds up. We've watched kids grow up and come back with their own families. We've seen farms we work with expand because they had a reliable buyer. We've heard from customers who drive past three other stores because ours is worth it to them.

We don't advertise that loudly. But it matters to us every single day.
