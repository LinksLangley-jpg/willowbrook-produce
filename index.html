Our Story

A Langley institution,
built on freshness.

For over 27 years, Willowbrook Produce has been the go-to destination for fresh, healthy produce in Langley. We believe that eating well should be easy, affordable, and rooted in your own community.

We source directly from local BC farmers, label every locally grown item clearly, and bring you seasonal produce that hasn't sat in a warehouse for weeks. Fresh means something real here.

Why We Buy Local

Supporting the farmers
who feed our community.

At Willowbrook Produce, local isn't a marketing word. It's a practice we've held for over 27 years. When you shop with us, your money supports real BC farmers.

Direct from the Farm
Your produce hasn't been sitting in a warehouse. It goes from field to shelf, fresher and longer-lasting.
Transparent Labelling
Every locally grown item is clearly marked. You deserve to know exactly where your food comes from.
Better for Everyone
Buying local keeps money in the community, reduces environmental impact, and tastes better. Full stop.
